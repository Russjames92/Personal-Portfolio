Hi! Thanks for Stopping By!

I was born, raised, and currently live in Northern California. I’ve been involved in a lot of different things professionally. While I love to solve problems in a technical sense, my passions are creative in nature.

After graduating high-school at the young age of sixteen, I went on to college for both Opticianry and music. Two very different worlds, but one was a job and the other a passion.

My aim was to find something to combine a job and my passion. Thus I began pursuing Graphic Design and now Software Development as a student at Thinkful. So far I love it. I’m really looking forward to the many projects ahead.
